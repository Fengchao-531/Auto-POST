Official Update

Lightweight Invoice Flow (Demo)

A streamlined demo covering the full cycle from manual OTP to Share & Archive, built to test human-in-the-loop + agent handoff.

What’s new

  • Manual OTP Verification: One-time code required to start the flow.
  • Preview Invoice: Inline “Invoice Draft” preview for quick review.
  • Download Invoice: Save a local copy (demo uses .txt; real-world is typically PDF).
  • Share & Archive: Close the loop with practical options:
    • Sync to accounting systems (Xero / QuickBooks)
    • Email to customer (deliver the invoice PDF)
    • Archive to cloud (Google Drive / Dropbox)
    • Generate reimbursement summary (one-page overview)
    • Update contact details (store userId & invoice header)
